Neon Nights

Genre: Sci-Fi • 9 min read

In a city glowing with digital rain, memories were currency and the rich owned their pasts. Kael, a rogue hacker, had nothing — not even his name.

He broke into the Memory Vault to retrieve what was stolen from him. What he found wasn’t his past — it was someone else’s love story, rewritten with his face.

As the neon flickered, Kael realized: in a world built on illusions, identity was the only crime worth committing.
