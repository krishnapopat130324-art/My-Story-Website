Whispers of the Forest

Genre: Adventure • 7 min read

When Rian wandered too deep into the foggy woods, he found trees older than time itself. Their bark shimmered faintly, like veins of gold.

Each whisper through the leaves wasn’t the wind — it was language. The forest remembered the footsteps of those who came before and offered guidance to those who listened.

By dawn, Rian realized the whispers weren’t leading him out… they were leading him home.
