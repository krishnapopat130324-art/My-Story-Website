Midnight Letters

Genre: Mystery • 6 min read

Every night at exactly midnight, a letter appeared on Elenor's doorstep — unsigned, ink still wet. Each one revealed secrets no one else could know.

She tried staying awake to catch the writer, but fell asleep each time. One night, she placed a mirror across from her door. At midnight, she saw herself — older, tearful, placing the letter gently.

Some stories aren’t written for others. They’re written for the future you, begging to remember.
